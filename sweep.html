HOW TO TRADE LIQUIDITY SWEEPS
◈ STOP HUNT
What it is: Price spikes above a key level (triggering stops of short sellers) or below a key level (triggering stops of longs), then immediately reverses. This is smart money grabbing liquidity before moving the other direction.

How to trade it:
1. Wait for the sweep candle to CLOSE back inside the key level — do not enter during the wick
2. Enter on the NEXT candle open (confirmation)
3. Stop loss goes just beyond the sweep wick tip
4. Target = next liquidity pool in the reversal direction

Best confirmation: Volume spike on the sweep candle + small body (long wick) = strong rejection
◈ EQUAL HIGHS / LOWS
What it is: Two or more highs/lows at the same price level = a liquidity pool. Stop orders cluster here. When price sweeps through them and reverses, it's a high-probability setup.

How to trade it:
1. Equal highs swept → price ran stops above, expect bearish reversal → short entry
2. Equal lows swept → price ran stops below, expect bullish reversal → long entry
3. Entry after the sweep candle closes. SL just beyond the highs/lows.

Key insight: The more times price has tested the same level without breaking it, the more stops are sitting there. A sweep of triple-equal highs = massive liquidity grab.
◈ ORDER BLOCK
What it is: The last opposing candle before a strong directional move. Institutions place large orders here. When price retraces to this zone, it often reacts strongly — this is your entry.

Bullish OB: Last bearish (red) candle before a strong push up → buy when price returns to that candle's range
Bearish OB: Last bullish (green) candle before a strong push down → sell when price returns to that candle's range

How to trade it:
1. Mark the order block zone (high to low of the candle)
2. Wait for price to retrace INTO the zone
3. Enter at 50% of the candle or on a lower timeframe confirmation
4. SL just beyond the OB. TP = the origin of the move that created it.
⬡ COMBINING ALL THREE FOR HIGHEST PROBABILITY
When a stop hunt occurs at equal highs/lows AND that level coincides with an order block — you have a triple confluence setup. These are the highest-probability trades the scanner can find. Wait for all three to align on the same ticker at the same price zone.
⚠ Always confirm with price action. Scanner detects setups — your job is to confirm with the candle close. Never enter during the sweep wick. Risk only what you can afford to lose.
